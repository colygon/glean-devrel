# Co-working Space & Open Office Hours

## Overview

Join the Glean team every week for co-working sessions and open office hours. Get direct help from engineers, collaborate with community members, and build together.

## Weekly Schedule

### In-Person (San Francisco)
**Location**: Glean HQ, San Francisco  
**When**: Every Tuesday, 2:00 PM - 6:00 PM PT  
**Capacity**: 20 people

### Virtual (Online)
**Platform**: Discord / Zoom  
**When**: Every Thursday, 10:00 AM - 2:00 PM PT  
**Capacity**: Unlimited

## What to Expect

### Open Office Hours Format

**2:00 PM - 3:00 PM**: Structured Q&A
- Ask technical questions
- Get code reviews
- Discuss architecture decisions
- Troubleshoot issues

**3:00 PM - 5:00 PM**: Co-working Time
- Work on your projects
- Pair program with others
- Ad-hoc discussions
- Team members available for help

**5:00 PM - 6:00 PM**: Social Hour
- Network with community
- Demo what you've built
- Share ideas and feedback
- Plan collaborations

## Who Should Attend

- **Developers** building with Glean
- **MCP creators** working on servers or clients
- **Contributors** to Glean open source
- **Grant/bounty applicants** seeking guidance
- **Anyone** curious about Glean internals

## How to Participate

### In-Person (SF)
1. **RSVP** on our community calendar
2. **Check in** at reception (mention Glean office hours)
3. **Bring your laptop** and questions
4. **Optional**: Prepare a 2-min demo of what you're building

### Virtual
1. **Join Discord** #office-hours channel
2. **Show up** any time during the window
3. **Use the queue** to request help
4. **Share screen** when discussing code

## Preparation Tips

To get the most out of office hours:

1. **Come with specific questions** rather than "How do I get started?"
2. **Share code/repos in advance** if requesting detailed reviews
3. **Document your issue** with error messages, logs, steps to reproduce
4. **Be open to feedback** and alternative approaches
5. **Take notes** and follow up if needed

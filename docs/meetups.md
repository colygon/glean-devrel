# Glean Monthly Meetups

## Overview

Join us for monthly community meetups to connect with other Glean users, learn new techniques, share workflows, and hear about the latest features. Whether you're a beginner or power user, everyone is welcome!

## Meetup Schedule

### Frequency
- **Monthly**: Third Tuesday of every month
- **Duration**: 2 hours (6:00 PM - 8:00 PM local time)
- **Format**: Hybrid (in-person + virtual)

## Meetup Format

### 6:00 PM - 6:15 PM: Welcome & Networking
- Grab food and drinks
- Meet other attendees
- Community introductions

### 6:15 PM - 6:45 PM: Main Presentation
- Topic expert or Glean team member
- Live demos and walkthroughs
- Interactive Q&A

### 6:45 PM - 7:15 PM: Lightning Talks
- 3-4 community members (5 min each)
- Share workflows, tips, projects
- Open mic style

### 7:15 PM - 7:45 PM: Hands-On Workshop
- Guided exercises
- Work on real examples
- Get help from organizers

### 7:45 PM - 8:00 PM: Open Discussion & Networking
- Questions and troubleshooting
- Connect with other attendees
- Plan future topics

## How to Attend

### In-Person (San Francisco)
1. **Register**: [meetups.glean.dev/register](https://meetups.glean.dev/register)
2. **RSVP**: Limited spots, first-come first-served
3. **Check-In**: Arrive 5-10 minutes early
4. **Bring**: Laptop (optional but recommended for workshops)

### Virtual
1. **Register**: Same link as in-person
2. **Zoom Link**: Sent 24 hours before event
3. **Join Discord**: For chat and Q&A during event
4. **Camera Optional**: Audio participation encouraged

### No Registration Required
- Watch live stream on YouTube
- View recordings later
- No interaction, view-only

## Speaking Opportunities

### Give a Lightning Talk

- **Deadline**: 1 week before meetup

### Suggest a Topic
What do you want to learn about?

## Community Guidelines

### Code of Conduct
- Be respectful and welcoming
- Help others learn
- No harassment or discrimination
- Ask questions - there are no dumb questions
- Share knowledge generously

### Photography & Recording
- Photos/videos may be taken for promotional use
- Let us know if you prefer not to be photographed
- Attendees should ask permission before recording others

### Networking Etiquette
- Introduce yourself to newcomers
- Exchange contact info respectfully
- No hard sales pitches
- Keep conversations inclusive

## FAQ

**Q: Do I need to be an experienced terminal user?**
A: Not at all! We welcome all skill levels.

**Q: Can I bring a friend?**
A: Yes! Just make sure they register too (capacity limits).

**Q: Is there a cost to attend?**
A: No, all meetups are free.

**Q: What if I can't attend in person?**
A: Join virtually! We have full virtual participation options.

**Q: Can I attend if I don't use Glean yet?**
A: Absolutely! It's a great way to learn about it.

**Q: Are recordings available?**
A: Yes, posted within 24 hours on our YouTube channel.

**Q: Can I present without being an expert?**
A: Yes! Sharing your learning journey is valuable too.

**Q: Will there be time for one-on-one help?**
A: Yes, during the networking and open discussion periods.

**Q: Can my company sponsor a meetup?**
A: Yes! Email partnerships@glean.dev for details.

**Q: How do I suggest a future topic?**
A: Use the [topic suggestion form](https://meetups.glean.dev/topics) or message in Discord.
